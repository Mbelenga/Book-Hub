About BookHub

BookHub was inspired by my passion for reading and sharing my thoughts on books with others. It started as a small idea among friends who wanted to keep track of their reading habits and provide recommendations to one another. As an avid reader, we often found ourselves discussing our latest reads and realized the potential of having a platform dedicated to book reviews and recommendations.

The timeline of this project began with brainstorming sessions and moved into a detailed planning phase. I worked tirelessly to bring my vision to life, integrating features that allow users to review books they've read, rate them, and recommend books to others. This project is also part of my portfolio for Holberton School, showcasing my skills and commitment to building meaningful applications.

I am proud to present BookHub as a community-driven platform where book lovers can connect, share, and discover new reads. Feel free to explore my platform and contribute your own reviews and recommendations.
